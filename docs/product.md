# Product Documentation: AI Representation Optimizer

## 1. Product Vision
The AI Representation Optimizer is designed to bridge the gap between traditional e-commerce stores and the future of AI-driven shopping. As consumers increasingly rely on AI agents (like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini) to find products ("Find me a good waterproof laptop bag under $50"), stores must ensure their metadata, descriptions, and policies are perfectly structured for LLMs, not just human eyes. 

This product provides e-commerce merchants with an automated, comprehensive audit of their "AI Discoverability."

## 2. Target Audience
- **E-commerce Merchants (Shopify):** Store owners who want to future-proof their traffic.
- **SEO Agencies:** Marketers adapting their services from Google SEO to LLM Retrieval Optimization (LRO).

## 3. Core Capabilities

### A. Store Audits (Trust & Policies)
AI agents prioritize stores that demonstrate high trustworthiness and consumer safety. The optimizer scans:
- **Return & Refund Policies:** Ensures policies exist and are detailed.
- **Shipping Policies:** Verifies clear delivery timeframes.
- **Privacy Policies:** Checks for standard data protection clauses.
- **FAQ Coverage:** Validates that the store proactively answers common pre-purchase questions.
- **Trust Signals:** Scans product descriptions and metadata for consistent branding, social proof (reviews/testimonials), and industry certifications (ISO, BIS, FDA).

### B. Product Level Audits
Evaluates individual products based on 23 specialized rules, including:
- Ambiguous or short titles.
- Missing images or missing image ALT text (crucial for multimodal AI).
- Missing standard Shopify categories.
- Lack of clear "Use Cases" or "Materials" in descriptions.
- Contradictory tags or metadata.

### C. AI Perception Simulation
Instead of just relying on rules, the optimizer utilizes Google's Gemini LLM to physically simulate an AI agent looking at the product data. It provides:
- **Classification Confidence:** How sure is the AI about what this product actually is?
- **Ambiguity Detection:** Flags if a product is confusing (e.g., an "Apple" could be fruit or tech).
- **Retrieval Score:** A 0-100 score predicting how likely the AI is to recommend this product for relevant queries.

### D. Actionable Intelligence (The ROI Dashboard)
Merchants don't just want data; they want to know what to fix first.
- **Composite Score:** A single letter grade (A-F) indicating the overall AI health of the store.
- **High-ROI Fix Planner:** Ranks issues by how much score improvement they offer across the entire catalog relative to the effort required.
- **Executive Summaries:** Auto-generated narratives explaining *why* the store is losing AI traffic and exactly what to do about it.
